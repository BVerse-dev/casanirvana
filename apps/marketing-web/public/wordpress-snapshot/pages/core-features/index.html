common product questions

General & Business Strategy

What problem does Casa Nirvana solve?
Casa Nirvana replaces fragmented community logs, chats and tools with connected resident, guard and facility workflows.
How is Casa Nirvana pricing determined?
Pricing is contact-led and scoped around community size, enabled modules, onboarding and support requirements.
How is Casa Nirvana different?
Casa Nirvana connects resident, guard, facility-management and marketplace activity through one community-scoped platform.

Design & User Experience (UX/UI)

Can communities enable different modules?
Role-focused interfaces prioritize clarity, accessible interaction and truthful operational states.
How do supported payments work?
Payment methods and service categories follow configured live policy, hosted checkout and provider-returned status.
What design principles guide Casa Nirvana?
Supported modules and role access are configured during the agreed community rollout.

Functionality & Features

Who is your target audience?
Residents, security guards, facility managers, authorized administrators and configured marketplace participants.
What third-party integrations are needed?
Only configured backend payment and digital-service integrations are included in launch claims.

Performance & Security

What key features must be included?
Visitor operations, requests, amenities, notices, messaging, in-app updates, marketplace flows and role-aware administration.
What success metrics will you track?
Authorized teams can follow operational status across visitors, requests, bookings, payments and orders.
What customization options are available?
Supported community modules and role-aware access can be configured during the agreed rollout.
How is operational access controlled?
Authenticated roles and community membership determine which supported records and actions are available.
